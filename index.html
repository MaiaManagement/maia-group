Un ecosistema de empresas construido para la costa Caribe

The Maia Group S.A.S. es un grupo empresarial con sede en Santa Marta, Colombia. Desde la costa Caribe colombiana, operamos un portafolio diversificado de empresas enfocadas en talento humano, servicios legales, bienes raíces, formación profesional, construcción naval, bebidas artesanales, comercio local y turismo.

Tecnología

Powered by Maia AI

Every brand in The Maia Group is connected through a single AI layer. Built on WhatsApp — the channel every Colombian already uses — and powered by Gemini, Maia AI handles lead capture, client follow-up, booking automation, and business intelligence across all 14 brands simultaneously.

This isn't a chatbot bolted onto a website. It's the operating system of the group: one AI backbone, shared across every venture, giving each brand enterprise-grade automation without enterprise-grade cost. When a customer messages El Sanatorio, Maia Realty, or Maia Legal on WhatsApp, the same intelligence is listening — qualifying, routing, and closing.
